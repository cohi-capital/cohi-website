Our Story

We're two brothers living in San Franciso. We are working on a startup focused on one thing: cutting energy waste.

We grew up in a country where energy was both scarce and expensive. From an early age, we were very aware that wasting electricity had real consequences. Over time we also came to comprehend the environmental cost it extracts from our planet. Needless to say, energy waste grinds our gears like nothing else.

By helping homes and businesses eliminate unnecessary energy use, we want to lower costs, ease pressure on the grid, and do our part to protect the environment. We want to do our part in saving our planet and your pocketbook.
